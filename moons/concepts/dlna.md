# DLNA

Digital Living Network Alliance. Media-focused service discovery, now defunct.

## What it is
- Built on UPnP for media streaming between devices
- TV finds media server, streams video/music
- Consortium dissolved 2017, specification now managed by SpireSpark

## Why Saturn rejected it
- **Media-only**: designed specifically for audio/video streaming, not general service discovery
- **Defunct**: no active development or adoption momentum
- **UPnP dependency**: inherits all UPnP's complexity and security issues
- **Wrong scope**: Saturn needs lightweight metadata exchange, not media transport
