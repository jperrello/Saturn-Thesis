Admin: The admin could be anyone from a System Administrator to a tech hobbyist. These are the people who set up and configure Saturn services they could be app developers that want AI in their systems and need a way to put it in there. There could also be people who are to provision access to AI on an institutional level rather that be a university or an office space. It could also be someone setting up a router or a Raspberry Pi inside their house for people who visit or their family to gain access to the same services they have.

Consumers: These are average everyday tech people who do not program at all or do in rare circumstances. These would be someone like a sister who is using a photo app to edit her photos and create captions.. It could be a grandma who has no idea how tech works. It could be a student that is going to class and solving assignments. These are consumers in the sense that they consume technology and they may consume AI services but they are not in direct involvement with configuring them or even thinking about how they work behind the curtain.
